12. Harry Potter and the Deadly Hallows Part2


Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 is a fantasy film based on J.K Rowling's 2007 novel of the same name. It starrs the normal cast, including Daniel Radcliffe, Rupert Grint and Emma Watson as the trio continue to find a destroy Lord Voldemort's Horcruxes to hopefully stop him once and for all.
